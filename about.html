About Us

J.R and Sons

Welcome to J.R and Sons, your premier destination for all things Royal Enfield in Kerala. Established in 1974 as KMPA in Trivandrum, our journey has been one of evolution and dedication to serving enthusiasts of the legendary Royal Enfield brand. Over the years, we transitioned through locations, finally finding our home in Ernakulam on MG Road, under the name J.R and Sons.

With over 30 years of experience, we proudly stand as one of the oldest suppliers of Royal Enfield spares in the region. As stockists for Royal Enfield, we provide a comprehensive range of genuine parts, ensuring that your beloved motorcycle receives nothing but the best.


At J.R and Sons, we cater to the needs of both vintage aficionados and modern riders alike. Whether you're restoring a classic beauty or customizing a contemporary machine, our extensive inventory and expertise have you covered. From retro to modern, from the old guard to the latest models, we fulfill every requirement with utmost dedication and proficiency.

With a team of experts renowned for their knowledge and passion for Royal Enfield, we take pride in being the go-to destination for enthusiasts across Kerala. Experience the excellence of service and the assurance of quality that only J.R and Sons can provide. Join us on a journey where tradition meets innovation, and every rider's needs find fulfillment.
